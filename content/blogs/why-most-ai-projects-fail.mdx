## The Real Reason AI Projects Stall

It's not the model. It's not the budget. It's the absence of a clear problem worth solving with AI.

## Three Things That Kill AI Projects Early

**No defined success metric.** If you can't measure whether the AI is working, you'll never know if it is.

**Wrong process chosen.** AI works best on repetitive, high-volume tasks with clear inputs and outputs. Not every process qualifies.

**No ownership.** Someone on your team needs to own the AI system post-deployment. Without that, it dies in six months.

## What to Do Instead

Start with one workflow. Define what "working" looks like in numbers. Assign an owner. Then build.
