## The Shift Has Already Happened

AI is no longer a futuristic concept. It is infrastructure. The businesses that treat it as optional today are making the same mistake as companies that treated the internet as optional in 1999.

## What Changes When AI Is Everywhere

When every business has access to the same AI tools, the differentiator is not the tool — it is how deeply you integrate it. Speed, precision, and personalization at scale become table stakes.
