## The Tool Trap

Most businesses buy AI tools. ChatGPT, Notion AI, Jasper. They get a productivity bump, then plateau. The tool becomes shelfware.

## What a System Looks Like

An AI system is not a tool you log into. It's infrastructure that runs in the background — processing data, triggering actions, and delivering outputs without anyone pressing a button.

## The Difference in Practice

**Tool:** You ask it to write a proposal. You review it. You send it.

**System:** A lead fills out your form. The system researches them, drafts a personalised proposal, and puts it in your outbox — ready to send with one click.

## Which One Do You Need?

If you're doing the same thing more than ten times a week, you need a system, not a tool.
