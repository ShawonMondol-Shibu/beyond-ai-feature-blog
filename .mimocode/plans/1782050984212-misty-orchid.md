# Plan: 2-Color Accent System (Teal + Orange)

## Color Roles
| Role | Color | Use cases |
|------|-------|-----------|
| Primary (brand) | Teal | Links, selections, eyebrow labels, card hovers, glows, focus rings |
| CTA | Orange | Primary buttons, submit CTAs, urgency signals |
